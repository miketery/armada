How Armada Works

A new model for firearms ownership. Purchase, store, and take delivery on your schedule — with cryptographic proof of ownership.

Three Simple Steps

1

Order

Purchase the right to buy — a voucher functioning like a call option with a $0 strike price and no expiration.

You're securing the item at today's price. No transfer occurs yet, no background check needed until pickup. Your voucher never expires.

2

Store

Your equipment is held in secure, climate-controlled storage at one of our facilities for as long as you need.

Insured, monitored 24/7, and maintained in factory condition. Store for months or years — there's no rush.

3

Pickup

Schedule pickup when you're ready. We finalize the purchase and transfer, ensuring all legal requirements are met.

Background check, paperwork, and compliance handled at the point of transfer. Walk out with your equipment, fully legal.

Cryptographic Ownership

Ownership and transfer rights are secured using public key cryptography. You hold a private key that proves your claim to stored equipment.

Digital Transfer

Your ownership key is delivered digitally. Use it to authorize pickups, transfers, or prove ownership remotely.

Physical Mail

Prefer something tangible? We'll mail your key on tamper-evident media to the address of your choice.

Backup option: If you opt in, standard identity verification can serve as a fallback in case your key is lost. This is optional — by default, only the key holder can authorize actions.

Already Own Equipment?

Deliver your existing firearms, ammunition, or gear to any Armada facility and we'll store it under the same secure conditions. Same cryptographic ownership, same climate control, same peace of mind.

Transfer paperwork handled at intake. Your items are cataloged, photographed, and stored with the same care as newly purchased equipment.
